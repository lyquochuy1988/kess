Kess is excited to announce

the first ever Kess All American Championship Tournament provisionally planned for August 2024 at Madison in beautiful Wisconsin.

The prize for the Winner will be a trip for two to the Awesome Maldives

Kess will pay for flights and accomodation for two, for five days at an island resort in the Maldives Islands, Indian Ocean.

One finalist will be chosen from each US State

These finalists will meet at Madison, where you will be paired off in elimination rounds, until one person is left standing.

We will be using a simple method to decide the finalist from each state

The first step is to sign up for the Kess Championship.

You can play Kess with any adult, friends, neighbours, co-workers, strangers, does not matter who it is

Not online, but in person. And post the video of the entire match, proving your win, on any of these three public platforms : youtube or twitter or facebook. The video must show the entire match from start to finish, your faces should be visible and the board should be visible at all times. Your wins will be tallied. And you can play against the same opponent only once for the purpose of counting your wins.

Insight vacations

The person with the most wins in each state by the end of June 30th midnight CST will be declared the winner in that particular state. The date and time the video is uploaded to the public platform will be considered as the date and time of the win.
